# Vieval Guide

Vieval helps you define repeatable evaluations next to product code, expand them across models and scenarios, and retain both human-readable output and machine-readable evidence.

## Start here

- [Why Vieval](/en/guide/why) explains the problems Vieval handles and where its boundaries are.
- [Getting Started](/en/guide/getting-started) runs a deterministic evaluation with no provider call, credentials, or model cost.
- [Core Concepts](/en/guide/core-concepts) introduces the objects and lifecycle used throughout the guide.
